## What I Discovered

### About Skill Architecture
- Skills are SKILL.md files with optional scripts/ and references/ folders
- oracle-skills-cli manages 26 skills across 14+ AI agents
- The template pattern: frontmatter metadata + usage + steps + ARGUMENTS passthrough
- Scripts can use Bun Shell for clean async bash execution
- Subagent delegation is the key pattern: Haiku for search, Opus for synthesis

### About Oracle Philosophy
- The 5 principles are not rules imposed from outside — they emerge from 128K+ lines of accumulated experience
- "Nothing is Deleted" is enforced at every level: git, filesystem, knowledge base
- The ψ/ architecture mirrors a real workshop with distinct zones for active work, stored knowledge, and completed pieces
- Skills themselves embody the philosophy: versioned, append-only, human-serving

### The Workshop Metaphor
The atelier metaphor resonated deeply because skill craftsmanship IS a form of making. A skill goes from rough timber (an idea, a template) through shaping (writing SKILL.md, adding scripts) to a finished tool that fits the hand (tested, documented, installed). The workshop preserves every step of that journey.
